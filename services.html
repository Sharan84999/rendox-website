Our Services

Rendox Epic IT Solutions offers a range of staffing and consulting services tailored to the needs of software-driven organizations. Whether you need short-term expertise or permanent additions to your team, we have solutions to help your business thrive.

IT Staff Augmentation

Scale your development team quickly with our pool of vetted software engineers, QA testers, UX designers, and project managers available for contract or project-based roles. We handle the recruiting and onboarding so you can focus on delivery.

Permanent IT Recruitment

Find the ideal candidate for your full-time positions. Our recruiters specialize in identifying professionals who not only have the right technical skills but also align with your company’s culture and vision. We manage the hiring process end-to-end, from sourcing to offer negotiation.

Executive & Specialized Hiring

Need a CTO, team lead, or a niche technology expert? We provide executive search services and help fill highly specialized roles in software architecture, data science, cybersecurity, and more. Our extensive network allows us to connect you with top-tier talent for critical leadership and expert positions.

Training & Upskilling Programs

(Optional Service) Equip your staff with the latest tech skills. We can organize on-site or remote training programs in partnership with expert trainers, covering modern programming languages, agile methodologies, cloud technologies, and other in-demand skills to enhance your team’s capabilities.
